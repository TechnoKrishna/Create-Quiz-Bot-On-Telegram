/////////////////////////////////////////// QUIZ BOT TELEGRAM /////////////////////////////////////////////////

Step 1 :- Search Quiz Bot On Telegram.

step 2 :- Enter /start in TextField.

step 3 :- Enter Title Of Quiz.

step 4 :- Enter Description. --> THIS IS OPTIONAL. Can Enter /skip

step 5 :- Now Click On Create a Question.

step 6 :- Now Enter Question And Option And Tick On Correct Answer And Also Can Add Explanation And Click On Create.

step 7 :- If You Want TO Add More Question Then Click Create a Question Again.

step 8 :- Then Enter /done To Finish.

step 9 :- Now Select Time For Question.

step 10 :- Now Select Shuffle For Question or Anwser.

step 11 :- Congratulations!!! You Have Successfull Created A Quiz Bot Of Telegram.

step 12 :- Now You Have Many Option Like Start Quiz Start Quiz in Group ShareQuiz Edit Quiz Quiz Status Use Option As You Want.

step 13 :- Thank You.
